APP DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

OUTSET - Major Project



For my final year of University, I created a mobile app called Outset.
The aim of the app is to help new Graduates plan their future by giving them access to advice and guidance, notifying them of events and providing a platform where they can communicate with their Career's Advisor & other Alumni. This information provided is updated by their Universities Career Department and available to access at any time.

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and I created the app using Xcode/Swift.
