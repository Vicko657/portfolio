POCKET LEARNER EDUCATION DEVELOPMENT SYSTEM INITIAL DESIGN

The Pocket Learner System was created by parents of a child who had learning difficulies, due to this may other parents/guardians were able to use the system to help their children learn.

I was given the task of creating an initial app design for their current system.

I used Adobe Illustrator to create the design.

The font used was Airfly DEMO
