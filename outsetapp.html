Who are we?

Outset, is an app for new Graduates who need help planning their future by giving them access to advice and guidance which will be provided by their Universities Career Department and available to access at any time.

Most graduates find it hard planning ahead, even after they have a graduate job, at some point they would need to think about what the next step in their career they are going to take.
