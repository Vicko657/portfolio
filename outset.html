Outset

UX / UI Design | Web Design & Development
(University Final Project)

2018 - 2021

Description:

Outset, the artefact was created in my final year in University. The artefact included a mobile app design, IOS app and website. The mobile app aimed to help new graduates connect with mentors and plan their future. Outset also provided advice and guidance, access to a careers advisor and the latest career events. The app was rebranded in 2021 to fit the new app design guidelines.

Software Used:

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and I created the app using Xcode/Swift.

Click Here for the detailed design process

The Problem

Career apps that are currently on the market do not focus on equipping students/graduates with the skills needed during the job-hunting phase. I.e. interview skills, mentorship, CV services etc. Most students/graduates have to rely on their university career department which means they would have to plan ahead in order to benefit fully from the services their careers department provide, before they graduate. As most students find it difficult to juggle their university workload whilst planning their future career path, it would be beneficial for them to have a platform where they can have access to this information on the go. In comes Outset.

Main aim for the App

The main aim of the artefact is to design and develop an app that will help increase employment amongst graduates by making it easier for them to have access to the information that they require, such as careers advice, mentors, job opportunities and skill development. The platform would only be for graduates and they will be able to retrieve this information on the go.

Older Version of the App

The previous app design was created in 2018. Although the design has some good features, such as the easy navigation and the unique buttons, the website design needed to be updated in order to fit in with the 2021 mobile design style. For example, the basic alignment needed to be refined as well as making the icons easier for consumers to understand. Furthermore, some pages had that was unreadable due to the colour combinations.
