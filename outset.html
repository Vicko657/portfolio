Outset

University Final Project

2019 - 2021

Description:

Outset, the artefact was created in my final year in University. The artefact included a mobile app design, ios app & website. A mobile app to help new graduates connect and plan their future. A platform providing advice & guidance, events and access to a Careers Advisor.
The App was rebranded in 2021 to fit to new app design guidelines.

Software Used:

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and I created the app using Xcode/Swift.

The Problem

Other careers apps that are provided dont really focus on making sure that the students/graduates are equipped for the job that they are applying to and most of the time the only help they have is from their career department. Most students find it hard planning ahead when they have left university, they are often unemployed or find it hard to plot a career path for their future. Students also find it difficult to juggle their university work load whilst making moves for their career path, therefore it would make it easier for them to access this information on the go e.g. an app. This showed that there was a need for a solution that will help students efficiently come to a suitable long term decision regarding their careers. This app I am planning to create aims to help them.

Main aim for the App

The main aim of the artefact is to design and develop an app that will help increase employment amongst graduates by making it easier for them to have access to the information that they require, such as careers advice, mentors, job opportunities and skill development. The platform would only be for graduates and they will be able to retrieve this information on the go.
