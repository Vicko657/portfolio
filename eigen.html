Eigen Technologies

Visual Design Intern

2020

Description:

Eigen Technologies is an artificial intelligence company that provides natural language processing (NLP) technology, with headquarters based in London and New York. As part of my role as 'Visual Design Intern', I worked closely with the Senior Brand Designer on various projects such as; wireframing, designing and creating prototypes for the company website, UX & UI design for business cards, social media campaigns and whitepapers.

Software Used:

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and Adobe Indesign.

Graphic Design

Eigen Technologies had a clear design guideline for their brand which I adapted into visual assets to communicate various different brand messages. I was able to experiment with different types of design techniques such as isometric illustrations, shapes, typography, images and layout etc. Below are examples of the designs I produced.

Web Design & Prototyping
