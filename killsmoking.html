Kill Smoking

Web Design & Development

As part of my module in university, I worked in a group to create the 'Kill Smoking' campaign. The main aim of this campaign was to highlight the negative effects that smoking can have on your health and wellbeing. I created the website for this campaign which included the design of the interactive infographics as seen below.


Web Design & Development

We decided as a team that displaying facts/figures on the website about smoking would be the most effective method of getting the users attention when they first enter the site. Therefore, I created the text slideshow you see when you first land on the page. This was further supported by the use of dark/smoky image illustrations to create a negative association with smoking in the users mind.
