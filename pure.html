Pure

University 360 Marketing Campaign

2019

Description:

The main aim of this project was to create a 360-marketing campaign pitch. The campaigns target audience were young girls/women of African origin with textured hair. The campaign sought to empower these young women whilst providing a solution in the form of a product to maintain the health of their hair.

Software Used:

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator & Adobe After Effects.

Branding & Graphics

As part of the brand strategy, I created different products which were advertised as a 3-step curly hair routine, as shown below. Each step included a quote which was used to communicate an empowering message about their hair. This helped create a positive relationship with the customer and the brand. The accompanying illustrations were used to mirror the products text. For example 'Step 1' focuses on the word 'fresh' therefore, the lemon/coconut illustrations were used to further portray this message.

Animation

Another aspect of the campaign was to create a promotional video to showcase the product. I created this animation using Adobe After Effects and repurposed the illustrations to create content for the video.

The Location

As part of the positioning for the campaign, I had to consider which locations had the most foot traffic from our target audience. One of which was underground stations in urban areas and bus stops near school districts. In order to visualise how the promotional material would look like in different outdoor locations, I created the mock-ups displayed.
