Pure

This was an Individual project the aim was to create a 360 Campaign Pitch

I used Adobe Illustrator to create the design.

I used a variation of fonts including Photophate Solid and Piepie

Research

A common problem that young african women face is their hair.

Mainly women with 4c 4b 4a hair

This hair texture is very fragile and needs a lot of moisture in order to grow

Some are clueless with what products are best for their hair

As an african myself, I often find it very hard to grow my hair, so I give up and often have protective styles such as braids, wigs etc…

Many people have followed this route and view their hair texture as a burden

My Idea

Promoting ethnic natural hair growth that is not represented in any mainstream marketing campaign in the uk.

Aka curly hair 4a 4b 4c !

From my research, most marketing campaigns emphasise that straight hair is pure & natural, with this campaign i am trying to change that view.

By empowering ethnic young girls/women who have this texture hair that they dont have to relax or texturise their natural curls.
