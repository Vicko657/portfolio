Digital Experience

2018

Description:

For my Digital Experience module I created A Healthy Energy Drink Campaign. This is a Digital Experience that aims to provide an alternative healthier energy drink for people who work daily, students etc... There is a range of five different drinks that are advertised which have healthy ingredients that boost their energy.

I designed the logo, website and the questionaire for the website had questions based on how people study, complete their work and their health.

Software Used:

I used Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator.

The Campaign

This is how the Digital Experience will be: There will be a range of five different healthy drinks that will be advertised which will boost their energy and there willl be also be a link to a webiste which will have a questionnaire. They can fill it out and it willl be based on how they study / complete their work and their health. Once it has been completed they are assigned a QR code, followed by a brief description of why they where assigned the drink and the location where they can pick up the recommended drink from an interactive vending machine. The vending machines will be located in several hotspots in London. They can use QR code to retrieve the drink and once they have recieved the drink their they would get the catered energy drink and also recieve a printed slip which will have information about maintaining your health in University and contact information. For the design and prototype the location of the digital experience I will be focusing on is a University Campus.
