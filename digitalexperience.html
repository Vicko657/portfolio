For my Digital Experience module I created A Healthy Energy Drink Campaign. This is a Digital Experience that aims to provide an alternative healthier energy drink for people who work daily, students etc... There is a range of five different drinks that are advertised which have healthy ingredients that boost their energy.

I designed the logo, website and the questionaire on the website which will asks questions based on how people study / complete their work and their health.

Once they have completed the questionaire they are assigned a QR code, followed by a brief description of why they where assigned the drink and the location where they can pick up the recommended drink from the interactive vending machine.

The vending machines will be located in several hotspots in London. They can use QR code to retrieve the drink and once they have recieved the drink their name willl be printed on th eproduct and they willl also recieve a printed slip which will have a motivational or positive quote printed on it.
