Gaia - Clinical Trials App

This was a group project, the main goal was to create a app for clinical trials.

I was the UX Designer for the project which meant I needed to create the wireframes and also create the UX Design.

To create the design for the app I used Adobe Illustrator.

The font used was Neuzeit Grotesk. This was my second attempt of designing a app.

To create the design for the website I used Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator.
