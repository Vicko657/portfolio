Vibe Rooms

Logo

2017

Description:

Vibe Rooms are a luxury lifestyle brand that walks the fine line between a laid back get together and a vibrant social event. The brand that had a very sophisticated feel to it which I wanted to embody in the logo. This is why I chose the silver curly font "Satisfy" and decided to use the letters for the logo. I also made it very simplistic and minimal with relatively plain colours to emphasise the professionalism.

Software Used:

The software I use Adobe Photoshop to create the logo.
