Drive

Digital & Experience

A digital experience aimed to provide a healthy alternative to current energy drinks on the market, for people who work daily i.e. students, workers, gym members etc. There were five different flavours of the drink which advertised the healthy vitamin ingredients used to boost energy.

The range of drinks will be advertised as a healthy energy booster, as seen in the poster. On the website there will be a questionnaire which customers can fill out. This will ask questions based on their study patterns, work regime and their general health in order to allocate the drink that is most suited to them. Once completed they will receive their results, a collection QR code, followed by a brief description of the drink, it's health benefits and the various locations where the interactive vending machines are placed around hotspots in London. The QR code can then be used to retrieve the selected drink from the vending machine, and an information leaflet containing tips to maintain your health in University and contact information.
