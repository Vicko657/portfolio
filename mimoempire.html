Mimo Empire

Freelance Work

2020

Description:

Mimo Empire is a e-commerce fashion company that offer luxury handbags at affordable prices. I developed the brand identity, design, logo, website and supporting graphics/social imagery.

Software Used:

The software I used was Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop.

Branding & Social Media

As a part of the brand identity development process, I created a logo that would align with the luxury theme of the company. This meant considering which fonts, colour schemes and overall aesthetics that would make the end user associate the brand with a luxury lifestyle. This resulted in the use of a black/gold colour scheme and the fonts, "Didot Bold & Regulator Nova".

Furthermore, I collaborated with the Head of Marketing to create and implement the digital strategy for their social media pages, in order to help drive traffic to the website and increase sales. This included creating visuals that were appealing to our target audience (females aged 18-35) and would encourage engagement.
