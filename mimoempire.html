Web design & development

Mimo Empire is a e-commerce fashion company that offer luxury handbags at affordable prices. I developed the brand identity, design, logo, website and supporting graphics/social imagery.

As a part of the brand identity development process, I created a logo that would align with the luxury theme of the company. This meant considering which fonts, colour schemes and overall aesthetics that would make the end user associate the brand with a luxury lifestyle. This resulted in the use of a black/gold colour scheme and the fonts, 'Didot Bold & Regulator Nova'. Furthermore, I collaborated with the Head of Marketing to create and implement the digital strategy for their social media pages, in order to help drive traffic to the website and increase sales. This included creating visuals that were appealing to our target audience (females aged 18-35) and would encourage engagement.

Web design & development

Mimo Empire needed a website to conduct their trade in a user-friendly manner, so that customers get a good understanding of the brand identity, whilst making the consumers journey to the checkout as easy as possible. The first stage of the website development was to map out what pages/information that needed to be included on the site.
After this a competitor analysis was conducted in order to gain key insights into the strategies they had used and how well they were performing. To help visualise the website, I created wireframes and designs which were shared with the marketing team for their approval. The website was then created using the content management system "Shopify".


Packaging

Packaging was an important asset to Mimo Empire as it was the first physical interaction that a customer has with the brand. The packaging was designed with Mimo's luxury brand identity in mind. Mock-ups were then created in order to ensure that the manufacturers were able to accurately visualise and reproduce the product packaging. There were multiple mocks up created, ranging in size, positioning and alignment etc.
